Gustave Eiffel, whose company designed and constructed the Eiffel Tower engraved on the tower seventy-two names of French scientists, engineers, and mathematicians in recognition of their contributions. The list is criticized for excluding the name of Sophie Germain whose work was critical to the construction of the tower. Some people attribute this to the fact that Sophie Germain is a woman.
